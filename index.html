John Cena - WWE superstar

John Cena is a well-known professional wrestler, actor, and television host. He gained fame in the world of professional wrestling as one of the top stars in WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment)

John Cena's list of achievements in WWE is nothing short of impressive. With multiple WWE Championship and World Heavyweight Championship reigns to his name, he has cemented himself as one of the most decorated wrestlers in the company's history. His charisma, dedication to the sport, and tireless work ethic have made him a fan favorite, and his "Never Give Up" motto has become an iconic part of his persona. Cena's contributions extend beyond the ring, as his philanthropic efforts and successful foray into Hollywood have solidified his status as a multi-talented and beloved figure in the world of entertainment.

Undertaker - WWE Superstar

The Undertaker, whose real name is Mark William Calaway, is a legendary professional wrestler who gained worldwide fame in WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment).

he is celebrated for his eerie and supernatural persona, unmatched in-ring skills, and an unparalleled WrestleMania winning streak that captured the imagination of fans worldwide. Brock lesner - WWE Superstar

Brock Lesnar is a professional wrestler and mixed martial artist who is well-known for his time in WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment)

With his imposing physique and explosive wrestling style, he became a dominant force in WWE, amassing numerous world championships and memorable moments. His return to WWE in 2012, coupled with his part-time status, has continued to keep fans on the edge of their seats. Beyond the squared circle, Lesnar's successful foray into the world of UFC, where he held the heavyweight championship, showcased his versatility and athleticism. His "Suplex City" catchphrase and his aura of unpredictability have made him a larger-than-life figure in the world of combat sports, leaving an enduring legacy.

Goldberg - WWE Superstar

Bill Goldberg, often simply referred to as Goldberg, is a former professional wrestler who gained fame in World Championship Wrestling (WCW) and later in World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE)

Bill Goldberg, often simply known as Goldberg, is a legendary figure in the world of professional wrestling. With an awe-inspiring undefeated streak and a powerful in-ring presence, he became an iconic force in both WCW and WWE. His signature moves, the Jackhammer and Spear, left a lasting impact on the sport, and his intense charisma captivated fans worldwide. Goldberg's return to WWE in 2016 created a wave of excitement, solidifying his status as one of the all-time greats in the wrestling world. His induction into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2018 marked a fitting tribute to a career defined by dominance, energy, and an unforgettable connection with the audience
